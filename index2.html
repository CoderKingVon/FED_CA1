TAKE URGENT ACTIONS TO COMBAT CLIMATE CHANGE AND ITS IMPACTS

Climate change is real and a pressing threat to the whole world. The effects are already manifesting itself in the real world presently. Acting now is crucial as it can cushion the impacts and ramifications of climate change.

Click on the "Learn More" button to find out what I am doing to contribute to actions to combat climate change. You as an individual can also follow the list below.

Learn More →

Saving Energy

I plan to waste less energy by:

  • Washing the laundry with cold water
  • Hanging clothes to dry instead of using a dryer
  • Switching to LED light bulbs and energy-efficient electric appliances

Taking Other Modes of Transport

Most mode of transports burn fossil fuels which produce greenhouse gases. There are greener modes of transports that I plan to take more:

  • Walking
  • Cycling
  • Taking public

Practising The 3Rs

We all have learnt this in elementary school. Reduce, reuse and recycle. For example, I plan to achieve this by:

  • Reducing usage of plastic bag and using eco-friendly bags
  • Reusing my older and smaller clothes to use a cloths to clean windows.
  • Recycling carboards I received from packaging of parcels

Reducing Food Wastage

Food wastage is one of the most prominent problem in Singapore. To do my part in stopping this, I will:

  • Taking away unfinished food and storing it for later
  • Making food compost for my plants in my HDB flat
  • Ordering less if I am not hungry

Switching to Electric Car

My family and I will plan to buy an electric car to move around when they saved up enough money. The benefits of using an electric car include:

  • Being energy efficient
  • Reducing greenhouse emissions
